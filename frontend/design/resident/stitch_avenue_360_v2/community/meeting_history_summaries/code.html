Annual Budget Review

May 15, 2024

The committee finalized the allocation for the upcoming fiscal year, prioritizing the renovation of the central park area and upgrading the community gym facilities. Key discussions involved adjusting the monthly maintenance fees to accommodate rising utility costs while ensuring a balanced reserve fund for emergency repairs.

Summer Social Planning

May 02, 2024

Exciting ideas were proposed for the 2024 Summer Solstice block party. Suggestions included hiring local food trucks, organizing a professional fireworks display, and setting up an outdoor cinema for the kids. A subcommittee was formed to handle catering logistics and neighbor permit approvals.

Infrastructure & Safety

April 18, 2024

The meeting addressed the recent concern regarding street lighting in the West Wing. Plans for installing smart LED lights with motion sensors were reviewed. Additionally, the road resurfacing schedule was shared with residents to minimize commute disruptions over the next month.
