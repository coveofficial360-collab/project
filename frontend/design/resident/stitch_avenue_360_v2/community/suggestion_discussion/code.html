Transforming the Rooftop into a Community Herb Garden & Lounge

Elena Rodriguez

Penthouse B • Resident since 2021

Our current rooftop space is underutilized, primarily serving as a storage area for maintenance equipment. I propose we convert the eastern section into a self-sustaining herb garden and a refined community lounge.

This initiative would not only improve our building's air quality and sustainability footprint but also provide a serene urban sanctuary for residents to connect. Imagine picking fresh basil for your dinner or working from a sun-drenched outdoor desk surrounded by lavender.

Key Benefits

  • check_circle Fresh produce for all building residents at no cost.
  • check_circle Dedicated outdoor coworking zone with high-speed mesh Wi-Fi.
  • check_circle Significant increase in property value and building desirability.
