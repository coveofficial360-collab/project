Annual Budget Review

calendar_today October 24, 2023
location_on Community Hall

summarize Executive Summary

The annual budget review focused on stabilizing the long-term financial health of the association while addressing immediate infrastructure upgrades. The board acknowledged a successful fiscal year 2023 with expenditures remaining within projected margins despite rising utility costs.

The most significant development was the unanimous decision to increase reserve fund contributions by 5%. This strategic move ensures adequate funding for major capital projects slated for 2024, including the high-priority roofing overhaul for buildings A and C.

Additionally, the transition to sustainable energy solutions was advanced with the approval of the smart-lighting initiative. This project is expected to reduce common area energy consumption by 15% annually, further strengthening our commitment to operational efficiency.
